About PadelPro

Est. 2020

Our Story

PadelPro was founded in 2020 with a singular vision: to bring world-class padel to Tunisia. What started as a dream between three passionate athletes has grown into the country's most respected padel community.

From our humble beginnings with just two courts, we've expanded to a state-of-the-art facility featuring 7 professional courts spanning indoor, outdoor, and the exclusive panoramic experience.

Today, PadelPro hosts over 500 active members, organizes weekly tournaments, and has become the go-to destination for both casual players and competitive athletes seeking the ultimate padel experience.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Fair Play

Sportsmanship is at the heart of everything we do. We foster a culture of respect, integrity, and healthy competition among all our members.

Community

More than a sports club, we're a family. Our community events, social tournaments, and coaching programs bring players of all levels together.

Excellence

From our court maintenance to our coaching staff, we pursue excellence in every detail to ensure the best possible padel experience.
